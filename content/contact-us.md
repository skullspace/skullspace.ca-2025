* * *

## Parking

Paid street parking is available in front of SkullSpace on the other side of Donald St. as well as on surrounding streets during business hours on Monday through Saturday. Street parking is free after 5:30 PM and Sundays.

On metred downtown streets, there is two hours free parking on Saturdays. You can pay for 2 hours upfront and get 4.

A short walk away, Notre Dame west of Hargrave is unmetred, offering time-limited free parking on weekday day times and unlimited free parking daytime on Saturdays and Sundays. Even during busy weekday nights with arena events you can still find a spot at this location.

There are also numerous commercial parking lots in the area including three of them behind us (off Cumberland ave) and across from SkullSpace at the Burton Cummings Theatre.

## Bus routes

SkullSpace is serviced by route 66, which stops right outside our door at the Notre Dame Southbound stop (#10667). The 17 and 29 buses also stop close by, and a large variety of buses stop on Portage Ave and Graham Ave near the arena.
