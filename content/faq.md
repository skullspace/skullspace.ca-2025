How much is membership?

Just $40 a month will get you 24/7 access to the space. Students can join for $20 a month (student ID card required).

How do I join?

Easy! Just come down to one of our weekly Tuesday night open meetings at 6:00pm and talk to one of the members. They can help you get started. For more information see our [Membership](https://skullspace.ca/membership/) page.
